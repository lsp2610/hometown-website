Visit Leigh-on-Sea

Enjoy the seaside and beautiful sights in this town that is a train's ride from London!

Top three activities to do in Leigh-on-Sea

Visit the pubs or bars

The Broadway pub located in the centre of Leigh-on-Sea is a great place to enjoy a drink.

Go for a walk in the parks

Library gardens offers great sea views and a peaceful area to relax.

Grab a bite to eat

Ugo's offers wood-fired Neopolitan pizzas from a family run business that has been around for over 25 years!
